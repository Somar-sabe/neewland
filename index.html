OVERVIEW

 

Damac SeaCrest at Dubai Maritime City offers a perfect blend of luxury waterfront living and work-life balance, with a range of 1 to 3-bedroom apartments by Damac Properties.

This residential gem is designed to capture the tranquil meeting point of the sea and a modern oasis. Its thoughtfully planned units provide stunning views of the surrounding port, the Jumeirah coast, and the vast Arabian Gulf horizon. Located in a vibrant, mixed-use maritime area, this city combines upscale living and business opportunities supported by ongoing infrastructure development.

With its modern architectural style, Damac Seacrest introduces a fresh way of living. The tower is a symbol of elegance, with beautiful facades and meticulously designed homes that offer premium layouts, spacious interiors, and quality furnishings. These homes promise comfort, style, and privacy, all while being close to the lively waterfront.

Location of Damac Seacrest

Come explore elevated seafront living at SeaCrest, located in the lively waterfront area of Dubai Maritime City, offering seamless connections to the entire city. Picture yourself in a permanent home by the water, nestled in this bustling maritime hub that blends modern residential spaces with industrial growth. Surrounded by the sparkling sea, Seacrest sits between Mina Rashid and Dry Docks within the vibrant Al Mina community. Just a few minutes from metro stations and Sheikh Zayed Road, you'll have quick access to the city's hotspots, including DIFC, Zaabeel, Dubai Frame, Jumeirah Beach, and the international airport.


SeaCrest by Damac Properties is a beautifully designed oasis that blends urban comfort with the serenity of the ocean.
